Only have five days? This itinerary captures the essence of Israel — its sacred heart, its desert drama and its Mediterranean cool — without feeling rushed. It assumes you fly into and out of **Ben Gurion Airport** and travel by train and a guided day trip. Got longer? See the [7-day](/itineraries/7-days-in-israel) and [10-day](/itineraries/10-days-in-israel) versions.

## Day 1 — Arrive & Jerusalem's Old City

Take the train from the airport to [Jerusalem](/jerusalem) (about 30 minutes) and check in near Mamilla or the centre. Spend the afternoon walking into the [Old City](/jerusalem/old-city): the [Western Wall](/jerusalem/western-wall) and the [Church of the Holy Sepulchre](/jerusalem/holy-sepulchre), with a first wander through the four quarters. Dinner at the [Mahane Yehuda market](/jerusalem/mahane-yehuda).

## Day 2 — Jerusalem in depth

Start early at the [Temple Mount & Dome of the Rock](/jerusalem/temple-mount) (limited morning hours), then trace the [Via Dolorosa](/jerusalem/via-dolorosa). In the afternoon visit [Yad Vashem](/jerusalem/yad-vashem) or the [Israel Museum](/jerusalem/israel-museum) for the Dead Sea Scrolls. A guided Old City tour pays off today.

## Day 3 — Masada & the Dead Sea

A classic day trip: a pre-dawn drive to [Masada](/dead-sea/masada) for sunrise, a short walk in the [Ein Gedi](/dead-sea/ein-gedi) oasis, then a float in the [Dead Sea](/dead-sea). Easiest as an organised tour from Jerusalem; back by late afternoon.

## Day 4 — Tel Aviv & Old Jaffa

Train to [Tel Aviv](/tel-aviv) (about 45 minutes). Drop your bags and head to ancient [Old Jaffa](/tel-aviv/old-jaffa) and the beach promenade, then dinner in [Neve Tzedek](/tel-aviv/neve-tzedek) or buzzing [Florentin](/tel-aviv/florentin).

## Day 5 — Tel Aviv & departure

A morning of [Bauhaus White City](/tel-aviv/rothschild) architecture and the [Carmel Market](/tel-aviv/carmel-market), or simply the beach, before the short train ride back to Ben Gurion. Allow plenty of time for airport security.

## Make it your own

Swap the Day 3 desert trip for a day in [Bethlehem](/jerusalem) or [Caesarea](/caesarea), or add days to reach the [Galilee](/galilee) and the north. Price your trip with the [cost guide](/israel-cost-budget) and read the [first-time-in-Israel guide](/first-time-in-israel) before you go.
