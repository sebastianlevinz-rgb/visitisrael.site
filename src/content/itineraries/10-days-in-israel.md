Ten days is the sweet spot for a complete first trip to Israel — enough to travel the country from the northern hills to the Red Sea without rushing. This route loops from **Tel Aviv** up through the north, down to **Jerusalem** and the **Dead Sea**, and finishes in the south at **Eilat** with a **Petra** day trip. Shorter on time? See the [5-day](/itineraries/5-days-in-israel) and [7-day](/itineraries/7-days-in-israel) versions.

## Days 1–2 — Tel Aviv & Jaffa

Arrive at Ben Gurion and train into [Tel Aviv](/tel-aviv) (about 20 minutes). Ease in with the beach promenade and ancient [Old Jaffa](/tel-aviv/old-jaffa); on day two, the [Bauhaus White City](/tel-aviv/rothschild), the [Carmel Market](/tel-aviv/carmel-market) and the [Tel Aviv Museum of Art](/tel-aviv/tel-aviv-museum), with nightlife in [Florentin](/tel-aviv/florentin).

## Day 3 — The northern coast: Caesarea, Haifa & Akko

Pick up a rental car and head north. Roman [Caesarea](/caesarea), then the terraced [Bahá'í Gardens](/haifa/bahai-gardens) and [German Colony](/haifa/german-colony) in [Haifa](/haifa), and the Crusader old city of [Akko](/akko). Overnight in Haifa or the Galilee.

## Day 4 — The Galilee

Around the [Sea of Galilee](/galilee): [Capernaum](/galilee/capernaum), the [Mount of Beatitudes](/galilee/mount-of-beatitudes), the baptismal site at [Yardenit](/galilee/yardenit) and lakeside [Tiberias](/galilee/tiberias). Consider the mystical hill town of Tzfat (Safed).

## Day 5 — Golan Heights & Nazareth

Drive the [Golan Heights](/golan): the [Banias waterfall](/golan/banias), [Nimrod Fortress](/golan/nimrod-fortress) and the [Mount Bental](/golan/mount-bental) viewpoint, with a winery stop. Loop back via [Nazareth](/nazareth) and the [Basilica of the Annunciation](/nazareth/basilica-of-the-annunciation). Return the car and transfer toward Jerusalem.

## Days 6–7 — Jerusalem

Two days in [Jerusalem](/jerusalem): the [Old City](/jerusalem/old-city), [Western Wall](/jerusalem/western-wall), [Holy Sepulchre](/jerusalem/holy-sepulchre), the [Temple Mount](/jerusalem/temple-mount) and [Via Dolorosa](/jerusalem/via-dolorosa) on day one; [Yad Vashem](/jerusalem/yad-vashem), the [Israel Museum](/jerusalem/israel-museum) and the markets on day two.

## Day 8 — Masada & the Dead Sea

Sunrise at [Masada](/dead-sea/masada), the [Ein Gedi](/dead-sea/ein-gedi) oasis and a float in the [Dead Sea](/dead-sea) — either as a day trip from Jerusalem or en route south.

## Day 9 — Eilat & the Red Sea

Fly or drive south to [Eilat](/eilat). Snorkel or dive the [Coral Beach](/eilat/coral-beach), visit the [Underwater Observatory](/eilat/underwater-observatory), or explore [Timna Park](/eilat/timna-park) in the desert.

## Day 10 — Petra day trip

Cross into Jordan for the unmissable [day trip to Petra](/petra-from-eilat-vs-amman) (an overnight is even better if you can spare it), then fly home from Eilat or back via Tel Aviv. Check the [border crossings guide](/border-crossings) first.

## Make it your own

Prefer to go deeper rather than wider? Drop Eilat and spend longer in the [Negev](/negev) desert and the Makhtesh Ramon crater, or add nights in the Galilee. Plan costs with the [budget guide](/israel-cost-budget) and start with the [first-time-in-Israel guide](/first-time-in-israel).
