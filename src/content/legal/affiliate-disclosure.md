**VisitIsrael.site is funded by affiliate links.** When you book something through a link on this site, we may earn a commission — **at no extra cost to you**. This is how we keep the guide free to read.

## How it works

Some links and "Book now" buttons on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and make a booking, the partner pays us a small commission. The price you pay is exactly the same as it would be otherwise, and a commission never changes which places, tours or hotels we recommend — our coverage is editorially independent.

In line with the US Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines, every monetised card on the site carries a clear, inline disclosure, and affiliate links use `rel="sponsored nofollow"`.

## Our partners

We work with the following affiliate programmes, grouped by what they cover:

- **Hotels & stays** — Booking.com, Stay22, Hostelworld
- **Tours, tickets & packages** — GetYourGuide, Viator, Civitatis, Abraham Tours, TourRadar, Tiqets
- **Flights** — Skyscanner
- **Airport transfers** — Welcome Pickups, Kiwitaxi
- **Car rental** — DiscoverCars, Rentalcars
- **Travel insurance** — SafetyWing
- **eSIM / data** — Airalo

When you follow one of these links you leave our site and complete your booking on the partner's website, under their own terms and [privacy policy](/privacy).
