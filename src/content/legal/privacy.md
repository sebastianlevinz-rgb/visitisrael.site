This privacy policy explains how **VisitIsrael.site** handles information when you use the site. In short: we keep almost nothing, and we do not sell or share your data.

## What we collect

The site is a **static website** with no login and no user accounts. We do not ask for, store, or process personal information to let you read it.

Like any website, our host (**Vercel**) automatically records standard server request data — such as the page requested, approximate region, browser type and timestamp — for security and to keep the site running. This is aggregate operational data, not a profile of you.

## What we do **not** do

- **No user accounts**, saved trips, or visitor profiles.
- **No advertising networks** — we do not run AdSense, Taboola, Outbrain, retargeting pixels, or third-party ad trackers.
- **No selling or sharing** of personal data with anyone.

## Affiliate links

The site is funded by [affiliate links](/affiliate-disclosure). When you click one, you leave our site and arrive on the partner's own website (for example Booking.com or GetYourGuide). At that point you are governed by **the partner's own privacy policy**, which may place cookies and run its own tracking. The partner may record that the visit came from us so a commission can be attributed — but that is handled on their side, under their policy.

## Your choices

Because we do not collect personal data or set our own tracking cookies, there is nothing for us to delete or export on your behalf. You can control cookies from affiliate partners through your browser settings.
