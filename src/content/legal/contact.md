We are a small, independent guide, and we read every message. Here is how to reach us.

## General & editorial

For editorial questions, factual corrections, or anything about the guides, email **[hello@visitisrael.site](mailto:hello@visitisrael.site)**. If you have spotted an out-of-date opening time, fare or fact, please tell us which page — corrections are a priority and we update the page and its last-updated date.

We reply to most messages within about five Israeli business days. Fridays and Saturdays are quiet, as that is when much of the country pauses for [Shabbat](/shabbat-guide).

## Partnerships & press

For affiliate-programme inquiries, joint-marketing proposals or press requests, use the same inbox with **Partnership** or **Press** in the subject line. Note that we do not sell sponsored sections or paid "do-follow" link insertions — every monetised link on the site goes through one of our disclosed [affiliate programmes](/affiliate-disclosure).

## Accessibility feedback

If you hit an accessibility problem — a keyboard trap, missing alt text, a contrast issue — please let us know via the inbox above or through our [accessibility statement](/accessibility-statement) page, and we will fix it.
