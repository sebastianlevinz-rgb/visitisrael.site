If you would rather not piece together trains, hotels and day tours yourself, a **multi-day package** is the easy way to see Israel — transport, a licensed guide, accommodation and the headline sights bundled into one booking. It is especially popular for first visits and the classic Holy-Land circuit.

## Who packages suit

- **First-time visitors** who want the essentials handled.
- **Holy-Land / pilgrimage travellers** following the Christian sites across [Jerusalem](/jerusalem), the [Galilee](/galilee), [Nazareth](/nazareth) and Bethlehem.
- **Travellers who would rather not drive** or juggle [Shabbat](/shabbat-guide) logistics.

## Small-group vs private

- **Small-group** — cheaper and sociable, fixed itinerary and departures.
- **Private** — your own guide, vehicle and pace; tailored route; best for families and special interests.
